Regression Equation: Win % = 0.53 + 0.0008 × Expected Points Total
R-squared: 0.243 — This means that about 24.3% of the variation in win percentage is explained by how many expected points a team scores.
In simple terms: the more 'expected points' your team has, the better it tends to do in the season. But it’s not perfect — some teams win or lose for other reasons too!
